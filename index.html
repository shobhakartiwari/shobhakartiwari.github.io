Who I Am

I'm an iOS Engineer currently working in the United States (CST timezone) with over a decade of experience building enterprise-grade mobile applications for Apple platforms.

I've led iOS development at United Airlines, building Inflight, MealOrder, and P2P applications using custom peer-to-peer frameworks with WiFi Direct and Bluetooth. Currently at Virtusa, I focus on SwiftUI reusable components and exploring VisionOS and Swift Macros.

Beyond coding, I'm passionate about giving back to the iOS community through mentorship on Topmate, writing technical articles on Medium (1.4K+ followers), maintaining active presence on StackOverflow (1.1M+ reach), and helping developers succeed in iOS interviews.
